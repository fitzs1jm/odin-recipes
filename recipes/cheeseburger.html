Cheeseburger

Description

Homemade burgers that put fast food to shame!

Ingredients

  • buns
  • ground beef
  • salt
  • onion
  • american cheese
  • pepper
  • fresh veggies
  • condiments

Steps

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties
  2. Grill burgers with salt.
  3. Add cheese and cover to melt when burgers are nearly cooked.
  4. Top with onions, fresh veggies, and condiments of choice.
  5. Enjoy!
