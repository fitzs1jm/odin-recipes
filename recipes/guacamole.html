Guacamole

Description

A delicious, healthy dip that is packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • avocadoes
  • salt
  • limes
  • onion
  • jalapeno pepper
  • tomatoes
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Mash your avacadoes.
  2. Finely dice garlic, onion, tomatoes, and jalapeno.
  3. Mix everything together.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and lime to taste. Mix again.
  5. Enjoy!
