Pizza

Description

This classic recipe will make you think twice about ordering delivery pizza!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • salt
  • crushed tomatoes
  • mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni
  • fresh basil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and salt to make the dough.
  2. Make dough into desired pizza shape.
  3. Add toppings!
  4. Bake in oven at 500 degrees for about 10 minutes, until the cheese is brown and bubbly.
  5. Enjoy!
